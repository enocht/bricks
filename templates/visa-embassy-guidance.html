Visa Requirements

Note: We will only list the documents generally required for study visas in most embassies. For requirements specific to each embassy, contact us.

  • Application form
  • International passport valid for at least a year
  • Copy of passport data page
  • Copy of School leaving certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • Admission Letter or Letter of Invitation from the school
  • Medical certificate issued within one month of visa application
  • Proof of Insurance
  • Bank statement from the sponsor to prove financial ability to bear the student's expenses throughout his/her stay in the country
  • Passport Photographs taken not more six months before the visa application
  • Proof of payment of Visa Processing Fee
  • Proof of Accommodation in the proposed country
  • Proof of Payment of Tuition Fees
